Iron Dome

A mobile all weather air defence system developed by Israel Aerospace Industries.

Iron Dome

  • Deployed in 2011
  • Intercepts and destroys rockets and shells in the air
  • Over 2500 rockets intercepted so far

Main technologies used within the iron dome

  • Detection and Tracking radar
  • Battle Management System (BMS)
  • Missile firing unit

Robotic Landmine Removers

Why do we need them?

  • Landmines are one of the major cause of death in the battlefield since WW2
  • They are long-lasting threats
  • 90% of victims are civilians

How does it work?

  • They can extract information from different sensors to guide soldiers around landmines
  • Vapor sensors to detect TNT residue

Landmine removers in deployment

The Mine Kafon Drone (MKD) and the Jevit
